Our Story

Magnetic Middle East Trading Company established in 1993, United Arab Emirates, Dubai by Mr. Nezar Alghanem. In 2020, Nablus Sanitary & Elect Ware TR LLC has been established with new inspiration, leadership with full power and wisdom of the founder of Magnetic, and by arms of younger generation leadership Mr. Qasem Alghanem to lead this new journey and rise it hand by hand to success. Magnetic Middle East Trading has been a key player in building material supply for construction and traders. Magnetic is the authorized agent and distributor of (Red Clay Block from Saudi Arabia). Magnetic has their own brands for sanitaryware from Italy & Turkey. Also, we have a variety of products produced in China in high quality and high specification.

  • Authorized agent and distributor of Red Clay Block from Saudi Arabia
  • Own brands for Sanitaryware from Italy & Turkey
  • Wide variety of high-quality products from China

Every product we offer is carefully selected to meet the highest standards in construction and trade.
